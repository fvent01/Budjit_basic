Purpose & Mission
Budjit is a self-hosted family budget planner and expense reviewer built and maintained by Francois Creative Designs. It gives households a single, private dashboard to plan monthly and weekly budgets, log and categorise every expense, track multiple income sources across family members, and monitor savings goals and debt payoff progress — all without sharing financial data with third-party services.

Beyond basic budgeting, Budjit includes a full analytics suite for reviewing spending trends, category breakdowns, and month-over-month comparisons. A calendar-based reminder system ensures no bill or financial deadline is missed, while import pipelines for bank statements, Excel files, and PDF pay stubs minimise manual data entry. The result is a complete, self-contained financial management tool tailored for the modern family.

Changelog

1.0
v1.0.0 — Initial Release
2024
First full release of Budjit. Includes the complete custom MVC framework, user authentication with role-based access (Admin, Parent, Viewer), budget management (create / edit / archive / duplicate), expense and income tracking, category management, analytics dashboard, and full settings pages (general, appearance, profile, password, users).

Plugins shipped: Savings Goals · Debt Tracker · Recurring Bills · Calendar Reminders · Financial Import (CSV / OFX / Excel) · Pay Stub Parser (PDF) · Plaid Bank Sync.
